Real Time Playing Visualization System of Biological Network

(Major: Biology)

Student: Wang Yi   Mentor: Lai Luhua, Zeng Zongyong

Abstract: Blooming of biological experiment data introduces various quantitative approaches to biology science research. Systems biology, with growing popularity, is an integration of theory, computational modeling, and experiment. There now exists a variety of simulation and analysis tools in systems biology communities. However, there still lacks software that can simultaneously display the dynamics of the species and flux of biological networks. I designed and implemented a real-time visualization system, by Flash Action Script 2.0, to show the dynamics based on the change of graphic size and color, thus to support the further study of biological networks.
Key Words: SBML, Flash, biological network, visualization
